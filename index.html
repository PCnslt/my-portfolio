About Me

I have more than 14 years in test automation and quality assurance, I’ve built and maintained frameworks that balance technical precision with a focus on real-world applications. At Delta Air Lines, I led the creation of TypeScript-based Cypress tests and coordinated QA teams spread across different regions, always working to improve our test pipelines and team communication. My work at the USPTO allowed me to refine Java-based automation and streamline testing across multiple platforms, while my time as an instructor at PeopleNTech let me mentor future tech professionals. I got my start at the Virginia Department of Health, where hands-on experience with HP Quick Test Pro and Oracle-based testing set the stage for a rewarding career that blends practical problem-solving with continuous learning and collaboration.
